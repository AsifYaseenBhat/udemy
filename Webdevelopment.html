what is web development

Web Development is a process that involves the development and maintenance of web pages as well as web applications that can be accessed and used over the Internet or via a private network connection. It includes processes and tasks such as web design, web engineering, web content development, etc. Besides, the professionals in this domain have to work with clients to help them develop e-commerce strategies and websites for their businesses. Web Developers have the liberty to take on all these responsibilities upon themselves and complete them on their own or they could work in collaboration with professionals specialized in distinct tasks. Web Development is a demanding and lucrative career option that is making its way across most industry sectors, including healthcare, business, government, education, and more. The websites developed by these professionals for their organizations allow end-users to easily navigate through what the companies have to offer. This way, companies can provide a better customer experience, increasing their sales and profits.
